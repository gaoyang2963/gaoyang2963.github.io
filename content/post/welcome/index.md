We are excited to launch our new website! The Virtual Intelligence and Graphical Animation Laboratory (VIGA Lab) is dedicated to advancing research in virtual reality, computer graphics, and their applications in healthcare and rehabilitation.

## Our Mission

VIGA Lab focuses on cutting-edge research at the intersection of:
- Virtual Reality technology
- Physics-based simulation
- Realistic rendering
- Physiological signal processing
- Medical rehabilitation

## Research Areas

Our current research spans four main directions:

1. **Physics-based Fluid Modelling and Simulation**
2. **VR + Physiological Signal (EEG/EMG)**
3. **VR + Rehabilitation**
4. **Realistic Rendering**

## Collaboration

We welcome collaborations with researchers, industry partners, and students interested in our research areas. Please feel free to contact us for potential opportunities.

Stay tuned for updates on our latest research findings and projects!
