Our realistic rendering research aims to produce photorealistic images and interactive visualizations:

## Global Illumination

- **Path Tracing**: Accurate light transport simulation for realistic images
- **Photon Mapping**: Efficient global illumination for complex scenes
- **Radiosity Methods**: Solutions for diffuse interreflection
- **Real-time Approximations**: Fast global illumination for interactive applications

## Material Modeling

- **Physically-based Rendering (PBR)**: Accurate material representation
- **Subsurface Scattering**: Realistic rendering of translucent materials
- **Hair and Fur**: Specialized models for fiber-like structures
- **Cloth and Fabric**: Material models for textile rendering

## Lighting Techniques

- **HDR Environment Lighting**: Image-based lighting for realistic scenes
- **Area Lights**: Soft shadows from extended light sources
- **Volumetric Lighting**: Atmospheric effects and light scattering
- **Adaptive Sampling**: Noise reduction for faster convergence

## Applications

- **Virtual Reality**: Immersive environments with realistic lighting
- **Architectural Visualization**: Accurate preview of building designs
- **Product Design**: Photorealistic product visualization
- **Film and Animation**: High-quality rendering for media production
