Our VR rehabilitation research focuses on developing effective virtual reality systems for medical therapy and physical rehabilitation:

## Stroke Rehabilitation

- **Upper Limb Recovery**: VR exercises for arm and hand function restoration
- **Balance Training**: Virtual environments for improving postural stability
- **Cognitive Rehabilitation**: Dual-task training combining physical and cognitive exercises
- **Progress Tracking**: Quantitative assessment of recovery progress

## Motor Function Training

- **Movement Guidance**: Visual feedback for correct movement patterns
- **Gamification**: Engaging exercises to increase patient motivation
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Automatically adjusting task complexity based on performance
- **Multi-sensory Feedback**: Combining visual, auditory, and haptic feedback

## Pain Management

- **Distraction Therapy**: Immersive VR experiences for pain relief
- **Relaxation Training**: Virtual environments for stress and anxiety reduction
- **Graded Exposure**: Controlled exposure therapy for chronic pain conditions

## Key Features

- **Personalized Treatment**: Customized rehabilitation programs for individual patients
- **Remote Monitoring**: Tele-rehabilitation capabilities for home-based therapy
- **Data Analytics**: Comprehensive analysis of patient progress and outcomes
- **Clinical Integration**: Seamless integration with existing clinical workflows
