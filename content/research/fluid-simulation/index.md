Our research in physics-based fluid modelling and simulation focuses on developing accurate and efficient methods for simulating various fluid phenomena. This includes:

- **Navier-Stokes Solvers**: Efficient numerical methods for solving fluid dynamics equations
- **Free Surface Flows**: Simulation of water, bubbles, and multi-phase fluids
- **Turbulence Modeling**: Capturing small-scale details in large-scale simulations
- **Real-time Simulation**: Methods suitable for interactive applications and VR environments

## Applications

Our fluid simulation techniques are applied in:
- Virtual reality training systems
- Film and visual effects
- Engineering analysis
- Interactive media

## Collaborations

We collaborate with industry partners and academic institutions to advance the state-of-the-art in fluid simulation.
